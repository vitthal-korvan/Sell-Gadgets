Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sell my old device?

+

To sell your old device, click the appropriate option on the home page, fill out the form with your device details and get a quote instantly.

2. What types of devices I can sell?

+

You can sell your mobile phone, laptop, tablet and any other electronic device. Just select the relevant option on the home page and proceed further.

3. How do I get paid after selling my device?

+

You can select from multiple payment options such as cash, UPI and Bank Transfer.

4. Do I need to include the charger or accessories?

+

No, we only need the device and bill. However, including accessories like the charger, case and original box might slightly increase your offer.

5. What should I do before selling my device?

+

You should backup your data, log out of all accounts and reset the device to protect your personal information.

6. How do I know the value of my device?

+

Once you fill out the form with device details like brand, model, condition and age, we will give you an instant quote.

7. Will I be paid the amount quoted initially?

+

The initial quote you receive is an estimate based on the information you provide. If the device matches the described condition when we inspect it, we will honor the original offer. However, if the device has significant damage or issues that were not disclosed, the final offer may be adjusted accordingly.

8. Can I cancel my sale if I change my mind?

+

If you change your mind, you can cancel the sale before the payment is finalized. Once payment has been processed and the transaction is complete, it cannot be reversed.

9. Is there a pickup service available?

+

Yes, we offer a free pickup service for your device. You can schedule the pickup at your convenience.

10. Can I sell a damaged device?

+

Yes, you can sell damaged devices, though the quote may vary depending on the extent of the damage.
